How to Upgrade to Premium

Unlock the full potential of our application by upgrading to Premium. You'll get access to exclusive features and enhanced capabilities.

Upgrade Steps

  1. Navigate to the subscription window within the application.
  2. Click the Upgrade to Premium button. This will open a secure PayPal payment link in your web browser.
  3. Complete the payment process through PayPal.

Once the payment is confirmed, your account will be upgraded to Premium instantly, and all new features will be available immediately.
