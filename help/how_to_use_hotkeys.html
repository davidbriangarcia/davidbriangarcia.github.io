Using Hotkeys to Automate Tasks

Hotkeys allow you to instantly run your recorded macros with a simple key press or combination, saving you time and effort.

Assigning a Hotkey

  1. Record a macro as you normally would.
  2. Once the macro is recorded, you will see an option to assign a hotkey.
  3. Click on the input field and press the key or key combination you want to use (e.g., F2, Alt+Q, or Ctrl+Shift+1).
  4. Save the macro. Your hotkey is now active.

Playing Macros with Hotkeys

Simply press the assigned hotkey (e.g., F2), and your previously recorded macro will play automatically. This is perfect for automating repetitive tasks in games, software, or any other application.
