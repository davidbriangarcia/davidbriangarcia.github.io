Record and Play Your First Macro

First use

Record a macro:

Press the Record button or the global hotkey, then press the Stop button when finished.

Save a macro:

When you press the save button, you can choose the name and location of the file.

Create the sidebar button:

In the configuration window at the bottom left, press Add. A macro (M1) will appear in the list.

Assign macro to side button:

When selecting macro M1, you can choose the assigned hotkey and other additional options.

Save and close:

Press Save and Exit to save the changes made and it's time to test the macro.
